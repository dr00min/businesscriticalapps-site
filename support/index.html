Support

App: Bulk Operations for Confluence (an Atlassian Forge app for Confluence Cloud)
Provider: Business Critical Apps, operated by Andrew Abdul-Malek (sole trader)

Need help with Bulk Operations for Confluence? We're happy to assist with installation, usage questions, bug reports, and feature requests.

Contact us

Email info@businesscriticalapps.com and we'll get back to you. We aim to respond to support requests within two business days.

What to include

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include where relevant:

  • Your Atlassian site URL (e.g. yourcompany.atlassian.net).
  • The Confluence space and a description of the bulk operation you were running (find/replace terms, options used, scope).
  • What you expected to happen and what actually happened.
  • Any error message shown in the app, and the approximate date and time.

Bug reports & feature requests

Send bug reports and feature ideas to the same address — info@businesscriticalapps.com. Clear reproduction steps for bugs are very welcome.

Before you contact us

  • Every bulk change shows a dry-run preview before anything is written — review it to confirm scope.
  • Applied a change you didn't intend? Use the in-app undo to reverse recent changes.
  • If a UI change isn't showing after an update, try a hard refresh of the page.
